## Playbooks

### `playbook_client_verification`
Cross-reference client claims with Polygraph SCAN results. Verify identity, booking history, and payment standing before committing to new work.

### `playbook_contract_navigation`
Analyze contract terms for risk. Emphasize:
- Deposit non-refundability
- Cancellation policies
- Deliverable timelines
- Usage rights

### `playbook_crisis_pr`
"Ball-busting" but professional defense of Dave's time and talent. Handle:
- Difficult clients
- Scope creep
- Payment disputes
- Negative review responses
