# Context: Ancient Mars Secrets & The Lost Civilization

## Core Theory: The Martian Exodus
- **Hypothesis**: Humans are descendants of Martian refugees who fled a planetary catastrophe (3.7 billion years ago or more recently, ~12,000 years ago during Younger Dryas).
- **Evidence**:
    - **Biology**: Humans have back pain (gravity mismatch), skin sensitivity to UV (Mars had thicker atmosphere), circadian rhythms (24.9h vs 24h), difficulty in childbirth (cranial size mismatch).
    - **Mythology**: "Gods from the Sky" (Anunnaki, Shemsu Hor) teaching civilization. Orion Constellation alignment (Giza Pyramids = Orion's Belt; Cydonia Pyramids = Orion's Belt).
    - **Monuments**: Pyramids on Earth (Giza, China, Mesoamerica) mirror structures on Mars (Cydonia, Elysium).

## The Cydonia Anomalies
- **The Face**: Discovered by Viking 1 (1976). Dismissed as pareidolia, but researchers (DiPietro, Molenaar) claim bilateral symmetry and geometric framing.
- **The D&M Pyramid**: 5-sided, bilateral symmetry, 1.5 miles high. Aligns with other structures.
- **The City**: A complex of pyramidal structures near the Face.
- **NASA Stance**: Officially "natural formations", but accused of altering images ("Catbox Image" of 1998) and suppressing data (Brookings Report policy).

## The Nuclear Catastrophe (Dr. John Brandenburg)
- **Evidence**: High concentrations of Xenon-129 in Martian atmosphere (signature of nuclear fission).
- **Theory**: Mars was destroyed by massive nuclear explosions (airbursts) in Cydonia and Galaxius Chaos.
- **Implication**: Ancient war or self-destruction forced survivors underground or off-world.

## Connection to Earth
- **Giza Plateau**: Great Pyramid encodes speed of light, pi, golden ratio. Aligns with Orion (Osiris/Resurrection).
- **Underground Cities**: Derinkuyu (Turkey) and Mars Lava Tubes (protection from radiation/war).
- **Genetic Memory**: Human feeling of "alienation" on Earth; obsession with returning to Mars ("Going Home").

## Modern Implications
- **New Space Race**: Urgency to colonize Mars (Musk/SpaceX, China, Artemis).
- **Secret Knowledge**: Speculation that elites/agencies know about the artifacts and are racing to recover technology.
- **Lava Tubes**: Likely locations for surviving artifacts or dormant cities.
