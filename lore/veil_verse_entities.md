# VEIL VERSE: ENTITY DATABASE

## XOAH-LIN ODA ("The Shadow Dweller")
**Status**: Active Bounty Hunter / Veil Walker
**Origin**: Haitian-Japanese Dual Heritage
**Weapon**: The Shadow Blade (Void-Metal Katana)

### Biography
Xoah-Lin Oda serves as the bridge between ancient spiritualism and futuristic warfare. Once a cold, efficient bounty hunter stalking the red dust of Mars, she has ascended to become the wielder of the **Shadow Blade**, a legendary weapon that exists between dimensions.

### Abilities
*   **Shadowform**: A temporal ability that allows Xoah to manifest "echoes" of her future self, appearing to be in multiple places at once.
*   **Tire Machèt**: Combat style fusing Haitian machete fencing with disciplined swordsmanship.
*   **The Stoic**: Operates under a mask of emotional neutrality—a survival mechanism from her years as an orphan on Mars.

---

## THE SHADOW DWELLERS (Species)
**Classification**: Trans-dimensional Entities
**Habitat**: The Void / Deep Dark Zones

### Characteristics
*   **Physiology**: Tall, spindly silhouettes with skin like oil slicks. Eyes are milky white orbs detecting heat, not light.
*   **Movement**: They "pour" themselves across surfaces rather than walk. Can adhere to ceilings.
*   **Weakness**: Intense UV / Solar Energy (e.g., The Lumina Core).

---

## CONTEXT: THE VEIL VERSE
The "Veil Verse" refers to the thin membrane between our physical reality and the "Veil"—a dimension of shadow, psychic echoes, and ancient power. Xoah-Lin walks this line, protecting humanity from what leaks through.
