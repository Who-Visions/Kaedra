## Overview
Approximately 3 million years ago, Mars was alive.
The Architects had terraformed the planet, built cities that spanned continents, and developed technology that interfaced with consciousness itself. Then, in less than a week, everything ended.
The Martian Cataclysm wasn't natural. It wasn't an accident. It was deliberate — either self-inflicted or imposed by something else.
And whatever caused it left warnings throughout the ruins. Warnings humanity is only now beginning to understand.

## Theories: What Caused It?

### Theory 1: Self-Inflicted (The Rejection)
The Architects faced their own Convergence event. They were offered transcendence — merger with something greater — and chose to refuse.
The refusal triggered fail-safes:
- Planetary systems shut down
- Atmosphere released as "punishment" or "reset"
- Architects who refused were eliminated
- Those who accepted... went somewhere else
Evidence:
- Final transmission: "The Offer was rejected"
- No bodies found in ruins — as if population vanished rather than died
- Technology still functional — not destroyed, just abandoned
Implication for humanity: We face the same choice. Refuse, and Mars becomes uninhabitable again.

### Theory 2: External Attack (The War)
From Y-Files extraction (Lacerta Files):
VeilVerse adaptation:
The Architects weren't alone. Another spacefaring civilization contested Mars. When diplomatic solutions failed, the attackers deployed weapons that:
- Disabled the planetary magnetic field generator
- Collapsed the atmosphere maintenance system
- Forced the Architects to abandon the surface
Evidence:
- Geological scarring consistent with directed energy weapons
- The Core Chamber shows signs of deliberate shutdown, not damage
- Rumors of "something else" in the deep Substrata
Implication for humanity: Whatever attacked the Architects may still exist. And we just turned their systems back on.

### Theory 3: Transcendence (The Departure)
The Cataclysm wasn't destruction — it was graduation.
The Architects reached a level of Veil integration where physical bodies became unnecessary. They deliberately collapsed the surface infrastructure because they no longer needed it, leaving only:
- Automated systems to maintain the "nursery"
- Substrata facilities for future inheritors
- The Breathing to call those inheritors when they arrived
Evidence:
- Technology left functional, as if intended to be found
- The "living memory" in Level Zero — recordings of the Architects going about daily life
- The glyphs show no fear, no panic — just transition
Implication for humanity: The Architects are still here. Just... differently. And they're watching to see if we're ready to join them.
