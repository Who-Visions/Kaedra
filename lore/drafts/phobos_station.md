## Overview
Phobos isn't a moon. It's a station.
The largest of Mars's two satellites has puzzled scientists since its discovery. Its orbit is impossible. Its density is wrong. Its surface features defy explanation. Every anomaly points to one conclusion: Phobos is artificial.
The Architects didn't just build cities beneath Mars. They placed an observation platform in orbit — a hollow construct designed to monitor their experiment. And after 3 million years, it's still watching.
