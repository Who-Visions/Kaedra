## The Activation Sequence

### Stage 1: Stirring (Weeks to Months)
Genetic: VRC genes begin expressing previously dormant proteins
Neurological: New synaptic connections form in observation-related brain regions
Experiential:
- Vivid dreams with consistent themes
- Increased déjà vu
- Intuition spikes that prove accurate
- Sensitivity to "atmosphere" in places

### Stage 2: Awakening (Days to Weeks)
Genetic: Full VRC expression; stress hormones flooding system
Neurological: Rapid neural reorganization; may cause headaches, disorientation
Experiential:
- Spontaneous visions (uncontrolled)
- Emotional overwhelm from others' feelings
- Physical symptoms: nausea, tremors, temperature instability
- Perceiving things others can't see

### Stage 3: Emergence (Days)
Genetic: VRC stabilizes at new expression level
Neurological: New pathways consolidate; brain adapts to Veil input
Experiential:
- First controlled use of ability
- Understanding of personal "type" (Seer, Anchor, etc.)
- Ability to "turn off" sensitivity (partially)
- Recovery from physical symptoms

### Stage 4: Integration (Months to Years)
Genetic: VRC expression becomes baseline; may pass to offspring
Neurological: Full integration of Veil-processing capacity
Experiential:
- Reliable ability use
- Development of personal techniques
- Understanding of limits and costs
- Risk: corruption if integration is incomplete
