# DRAFT: Project APEX — Syndicate Weaponization Program

> [!NOTE]

## Overview
The Syndicate doesn't just want to understand the Veil. They want to weaponize it.
Project APEX (Advanced Paranormal Exploitation eXperiments) is the Syndicate's umbrella program for all Veil-based military applications. Like Earth's DARPA, APEX operates with unlimited funding, zero oversight, and complete moral flexibility. Unlike DARPA, APEX has access to alien technology and human test subjects who can manipulate reality.
The results are terrifying.
